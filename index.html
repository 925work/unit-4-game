WoW Gem Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four gems below. By clicking on a gems, you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each gem is random and hidden until you click on it. Good luck!
